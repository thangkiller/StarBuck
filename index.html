EARNING STARS

Stars cannot be earned on purchases of alcohol, Starbucks Cards or Starbucks Card reloads.

Earn 1 Star per $1 spent when you can your member barcode in the app, then pay with cash, credit/debit cards or mobile wallets at participating stores. You can also earn 1 Star per $1 spent when you link a payment method and pay directly through the app.

Earn 2 Stars per $1 spent when you load funds and pay with your digital Starbucks Card in the app. You can also earn 2 Stars per $1 spent when you pay in-person at a participating store with your registered physical Starbucks Card or scan your member barcode in the app, and then use any physical Starbucks Card (regardless of whether it is registered) to complete the purchase.
